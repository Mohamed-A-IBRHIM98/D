The New El Mahmoudia Axis Project Overview

Project Details

Location: Alexandria, Egypt

Total Area: 60 Acres

Features: Luxury apartments, villas, parks and bullding.

Completion Date: December 2024

Developer: SAUDI EGYPTIAN DEVELOPERS

Contractor: Tabarak for Construction and Infrastructure

The project is divided into two sectors:First Sector: The open sector, with a length of 7.75 kilometers, starts from the Coastal Road Bridge intersection at kilometer 55 of the Mahmoudia Canal and extends to the Sidi Bishr Water Station at kilometer 62.7 of the Mahmoudia Canal. It includes 5 traffic lanes in each direction on both sides of the open sector, and the development of 27 intake points for raw water on both sides of the canal.Second Sector: The covered sector extends from the Sidi Bishr Water Station at kilometer 62.7 of the Mahmoudia Canal to the outlet in Dekheila at kilometer 77.1 of the Mahmoudia Canal, with a length of 14.3 kilometers. This is achieved by constructing a main water weir to control the water flow for two concrete water pipelines operating by gravity, with a total length of about 30 kilometers to the outlet. The two pipelines include one for irrigation water and rainwater drainage, and the second as an emergency line in case of water level rise. Additionally, two polyethylene pipelines are installed, operating by pumping to feed the water stations, and a perforated polyethylene pipeline for groundwater drainage, with a total length of about 13 kilometers.Moreover, the project involved relocating and upgrading the utilities along the project length, implementing a power line for the main wastewater treatment plant in Alexandria, and upgrading the siphon that supplies the Sharq Water Station. Asphalt roadworks were also completed, covering a total of 850,000 square meters, along with the main lighting network, which includes 3,300 lighting poles.Additionally, 5 surface vehicle bridges were constructed for U-turns to connect both sides of the axis and to create a corniche for residents along the sector.The road also includes the construction of 22 pedestrian bridges with a distinctive architectural style to connect both sides of the axis, with a total length of 1,360 meters.
